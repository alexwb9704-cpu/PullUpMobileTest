Frequently Asked Questions

What are your prices?

Automotive tinting starts at just $75. Commercial and residential projects are priced based on size and scope. Call us for a free, no-obligation quote.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes! We understand urgency and do our best to accommodate same-day requests when possible. Call us to check availability.

Do you offer mobile service?

Absolutely. We can come to your home, office, or any convenient location throughout Columbia and surrounding areas.

What does the lifetime warranty cover?

Our lifetime warranty covers bubbling, peeling, discoloration, and adhesion failure under normal use conditions.

How long does installation take?

Most automotive jobs take 2-3 hours. Commercial and residential projects vary based on size but we work efficiently to minimize disruption.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Columbia, SC and surrounding areas including Irmo, Lexington, West Columbia, Cayce, Forest Acres, and more. Call to confirm service in your area.
